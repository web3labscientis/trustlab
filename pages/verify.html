How to Verify

Using Verification Code:

  1. 1. Enter the 8-character code provided by your healthcare provider
  2. 2. Click "Verify Result" to check authenticity
  3. 3. View your verified medical test results

Using QR Code:

  1. 1. Click "Start QR Scanner" to activate your camera
  2. 2. Point your camera at the QR code
  3. 3. The system will automatically verify the result
  4. 4. Complete payment if required
