Instructions for Healthcare Providers

1. Connect Wallet

Connect your HashPack wallet to sign transactions and upload results to the Hedera blockchain.

2. Upload Results

Fill out the form with patient and test information. The system will generate a secure hash and store it on the blockchain.

3. Share with Patient

Provide the generated verification code or QR code to the patient for secure result verification.
