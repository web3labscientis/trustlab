How TrustaLab Works

A step-by-step breakdown of our verification process

1

Healthcare Provider Upload

Hospitals and laboratories securely upload patient test results through our admin interface. All data is encrypted and processed according to healthcare privacy standards.

Encrypted Upload HIPAA Compliant Multi-format Support
2

Blockchain Storage

A cryptographic hash of the result is stored on the Hedera ledger, creating an immutable record. The system generates a unique verification code and QR code for easy access.

Hedera Ledger Cryptographic Hash QR Generation
3

Patient Verification

Patients can verify their results using the unique verification code or by scanning the QR code. The system instantly validates the authenticity against the blockchain record.

QR Scanner Instant Verification Mobile Friendly
4

Secure Payment

Patients can make payments for services using HBAR or stablecoins through their connected HashPack wallet. All transactions are recorded on the blockchain for complete transparency.

HBAR Payments Stablecoin Support HashPack Integration
